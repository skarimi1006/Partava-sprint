Place the following 6 font files in this directory:

  IBMPlexSans-Light.woff2
  IBMPlexSans-Regular.woff2
  IBMPlexSans-Medium.woff2
  IBMPlexSans-SemiBold.woff2
  IBMPlexMono-Regular.woff2
  IBMPlexMono-SemiBold.woff2

Download source (free, open source):
  https://github.com/IBM/plex/releases
  → Download "OpenType.zip"
  → Inside: IBM-Plex-Sans/fonts/complete/woff2/
             IBM-Plex-Mono/fonts/complete/woff2/

After placing the files, restart the server.
The app will then serve fonts locally — no internet needed.
All devices on LAN will load fonts from your own server.
